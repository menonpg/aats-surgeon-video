# Video Brief — AI for Surgeons: A Practical Toolkit

## Purpose
A 15-minute narrated video overview for the AATS Annual Meeting 2026 workshop.
Aimed at cardiothoracic surgeons with little or no prior AI experience.

## Audience
- Cardiothoracic surgeons
- No assumed AI background
- Interested in practical, immediately usable tools

## Tone
- Authoritative but accessible
- 1 speaker (Prahlad Menon)
- No jargon without explanation
- Real demos / screenshots over abstract diagrams

## Structure
1. Introduction & AI Landscape (what's happening, why now) — 2 min
2. Interacting with LLMs — comparing frontier models, prompting — 2.5 min
3. AI for Literature Search & Research — 1.5 min
4. AI for the Practicing Surgeon (scribes, notes, codes, letters, grants) — 2 min
5. RAG / RLM — Your Own Knowledge Base — 2 min
6. Medical Imaging AI (VLMs, SAM2, BrainIAC, APOLLO) — 2 min
7. Data Analysis & Presentations — 1 min
8. AI Voice Agents & Conversational Risk Assessment (AATS AQG demo) — 1.5 min
9. What's Next / Call to Action — 0.5 min

Total: ~15 min
