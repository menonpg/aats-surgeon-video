Prompt Engineering

Structure your prompt. Transform your result.

THE 5-PART FRAMEWORK
ROLEYou are a thoracic surgeon attending at a Level I trauma center. CONTEXT67-year-old, 1.2cm RLL nodule, ex-smoker, PET negative. TASKWrite a one-page patient letter explaining the finding. FORMATPlain language, 7th-grade reading level, 3 paragraphs max. CONSTRAINTSInclude: finding, follow-up rationale, and next steps.
→ Send-ready output. Every time.
CHAIN-OF-THOUGHT (CoT)
Add "Think step by step." before your answer request. Forces the model to reason before concluding — dramatically reduces errors on clinical questions, differentials, and statistical interpretation.
"Think step by step. What are the most likely causes of this CT finding in a patient with…?"
FEW-SHOT PROMPTING
Provide 2–3 examples of input → desired output before your actual request. Teaches the model your style, format, and clinical vocabulary without fine-tuning.
Example 1: [operative note] → [structured summary]
Example 2: [operative note] → [structured summary]
Now do this one: [your note]
SYSTEM PROMPTS & PERSONAS
In ChatGPT Custom Instructions, Claude Projects, or the API system field — define a persistent role that applies to every message. Build your own surgical AI with your institution's protocols baked in.
ITERATIVE REFINEMENT
Draft → Critique → Revise. Ask it to critique its own output: "What's missing or potentially incorrect above?"

Generative AI for 3D Vascular Reconstruction

RF48 · Fontan Geometries from 2D Contrast-Enhanced X-Ray Fluoroscopy · Prahlad Menon, PhD

THE CLINICAL PROBLEM
Failing Fontan patients require precise 3D vascular geometry for pre-operative planning and computational fluid dynamics (CFD). Traditional 3D reconstruction requires dedicated CT/MRI — often unavailable at time of re-intervention.
THE AI PIPELINE
2D Angiogram → Gemini Flash 2.5
Vascular enhancement + segmentation → Hunyuan3D
3D geometry synthesis → STL / OBJ / GLB
CFD-ready mesh
🔗 github.com/menonpg/fontan-ai-reconstruction →
WHY THIS MATTERS
  • Works with standard 2D fluoroscopy — no dedicated 3D imaging required
  • Prompt-driven reconstruction — surgeon guides AI interactively
  • Output feeds directly into hemodynamic simulation (CFD) tools
  • Fully open-source — reproducible and extensible
MODELS USED
Gemini Flash 2.5
Medical image preprocessing + segmentation
Hunyuan3D-2.0
Image → 3D mesh generation (open-source)
Veo-3
Video reconstruction visualization
