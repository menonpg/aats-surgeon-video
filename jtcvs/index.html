Opportunities

AI makes every researcher more productive. Here's how.

⚡ SPEED & SCALE
  • Screen 200 papers in minutes, not weeks
  • Generate structured abstracts from methods + results
  • Produce publication-ready figures from raw data instantly
  • Respond to reviewer comments faster with AI assistance
🌍 GLOBAL ACCESS
  • Researchers in low-resource settings get same tools
  • Language translation removes non-English barriers
  • Video libraries become searchable, accessible globally
  • Open-source pipelines democratize advanced analysis
📖 NEW PUBLICATION FORMATS
  • AI-generated surgical video summaries with searchable transcripts
  • Interactive figures and dynamic data visualization
  • Living documents that update as evidence evolves
  • Multimodal papers: text + video + 3D model in one submission
🔍 PEER REVIEW ENHANCEMENT
  • AI flagging of statistical errors before reviewer sees manuscript
  • Automatic citation verification
  • Bias detection in study design
  • Consistency checks across methods, results, and conclusions
Pitfalls

The risks are real. The field is moving faster than policy.

⛔ FABRICATION & HALLUCINATION
LLMs confidently fabricate citations, invent statistics, and hallucinate author names. In biomedical literature, this causes direct patient harm if unchecked. Always verify every citation.
📝 AUTHORSHIP INTEGRITY
Who is the author of an AI-assisted paper? ICMJE: AI cannot be an author. But undisclosed AI use is now widespread. Journals are racing to develop disclosure standards. Transparency is non-negotiable.
💬 HOMOGENIZATION OF SCIENCE
When everyone uses the same AI tools trained on the same corpora, scientific writing converges. Novel ideas get smoothed into familiar framings. Use AI as a tool, not a ghostwriter.
🕵️ PEER REVIEW GAMING
AI can write convincing-sounding peer reviews. Predatory journals are already using AI to rubber-stamp submissions. The integrity of the review system depends on human accountability. Know your reviewer.
The bottom line: AI is a force multiplier for research productivity. Its risks are manageable with transparency, verification, and journal policy. The field that adapts thoughtfully will lead.
