The Clinical Problem

Single-ventricle hearts. Complex decisions. Inadequate tools.

THE FONTAN CIRCULATION
Children born with single-ventricle defects undergo the Fontan procedure — surgically rerouting blood to bypass the missing chamber. Over time, many develop failing Fontan syndrome: elevated pressures, reduced output, multi-organ failure.
THE BOTTLENECK
Surgeons need 3D vascular geometry for pre-op planning and CFD simulation. Traditional pipeline requires dedicated MRI/CT — often unavailable — plus weeks of expert manual segmentation and meshing.
TRADITIONAL WORKFLOW
✗Manual segmentation: days of expert time
✗Dedicated MRI/CT: expensive, often unavailable
✗Scale challenge: neonatal structures in millimeters
✗Volumetric meshing + CFD setup: specialized expertise
✗Total time: weeks — treatment windows close
"We had the knowledge to help, but not the means to reach the children who needed it most." — The access gap this work closes.
The Solution

From 2D X-Ray to 3D Heart Model in 15 Minutes.

THE 16-STEP AI PIPELINE
Steps 1–5: Gemini Flash 2.5 examines the fluoroscopic angiogram, enhances vascular structures, reveals hidden detail
Steps 6–10: Artifact removal, contrast enhancement, geometric optimization — iterative quality control
Steps 11–16: Final optimization → high-quality 2D projection ready for 3D conversion
3D Conversion: Hunyuan3D-2mini (384M-parameter diffusion transformer) → STL/OBJ/GLB
